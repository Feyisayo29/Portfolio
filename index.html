Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions clients ask before and after starting a project.

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. For a standard website, it typically takes 1–4 weeks from concept to launch. However, larger or more complex projects may take longer. I always provide a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your design process like?

My design process is collaborative and client-focused. It starts with understanding your brand, goals, and target audience. Throughout the process, I ensure open communication to make sure the final product aligns with your vision.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! Every website I design is fully responsive, meaning it will look and function perfectly on all devices—whether it's a desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Ensuring a seamless experience across platforms is a top priority.

Do you offer ongoing support after the website is launched?

Yes, I offer post-launch support to ensure everything runs smoothly. Whether you need updates, maintenance, or additional features, I’m here to help. I also offer training if you want to manage the site yourself.
